## Company

Owner: Matan Jacobson

**EDDI Technologies** — AI-driven educational systems and mentor-like learning flows.

---

## The Product Being Built
**Digitech Pedagogic Content OS** — a content factory that receives a brief and produces a complete course package.

### Current Version
K12 Edition — school-aged learners, Hebrew-primary content.

### Future Version
Adult Edition — organizations, business owners, professional training.

---

## Business Logic

### Why This Exists
Digitech cannot scale course production manually. One person holding knowledge in their head is not a system. This OS makes production fast, consistent, and handoff-ready.

### Revenue Connection
Every course produced = a sellable product for schools or a training product for clients. Speed of production directly affects business capacity.

### Strategic Position
Digitech is positioned as a builder of hands-on, interactive tech education — not textbooks, not passive video. This must be reflected in every course asset.

---

## What Must Always Be True

1. Every course can be run by a teacher who didn't design it
2. Every course has at least one interactive, game-like element
3. Every course produces a tangible student output
4. Production time per course: target 3–5 working days
5. All student-facing tools are free (Google Sheets, Flourish, Canva, Kaggle, Wordwall)
