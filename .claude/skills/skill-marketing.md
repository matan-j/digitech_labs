# skill-marketing

**Trigger:** STEP 5 of pipeline, or `/generate-asset marketing`
**Purpose:** Produce marketing and positioning assets for the course.

---

## Inputs
- `Course_Brief.md`
- `02_Curriculum/OnePager_Product.md`
- `02_Curriculum/Syllabus.md`

---

## Execution Steps

### 1. Write Marketing_OnePager.md

Target reader: school principal or program manager
Length: fits on one printed page (400–600 words)

```markdown
# [Course Title] — Program Overview

## The Problem
[1–2 sentences: what gap does this course fill for schools]

## What Students Will Do
[3 bullet points — outcomes described in student-friendly action terms]

## What Makes This Different
[2–3 points that differentiate this from generic tech courses]

## Final Student Output
[The tangible thing students produce — makes the course real to the buyer]

## Who Is It For
[Grade, level, class type, recommended group size]

## What's Included
[List: sessions, worksheets, game, teacher guide, etc.]

## What You Need
[Equipment, internet, prerequisites — be honest]

## Pricing / Contact
[Leave as placeholder or per brief]
```

### 2. Write Video_Script.md

60–90 second spoken script (150–225 words):

```markdown
# Video Script — [Course Title]

## Format: [Talking head / Voiceover / Screen record]
## Duration: [60 / 75 / 90 sec]

---

[OPENING — 10 sec]
[Hook — a question or fact that creates urgency or curiosity]

[PROBLEM — 10–15 sec]
[What students lack or what teachers struggle to teach]

[SOLUTION — 20–25 sec]
[What this course does — specific, not generic]

[PROOF / EXAMPLE — 15 sec]
[One concrete example of what a student does or produces]

[CTA — 10 sec]
[Next step — contact, sign up, pilot]

---
**Speaker notes:** [Any notes for on-camera delivery]
**Visual cues:** [What to show on screen at each point]
```

### 3. Write Social_Assets.md

3 social posts for different contexts:

```markdown
# Social Assets — [Course Title]

## Post 1 — LinkedIn (school/B2B audience)
[150–200 words, professional tone, teacher/principal reader]

## Post 2 — Instagram/Facebook (parent or teen audience)
[50–80 words, engaging, visual-led description, can use light formatting]

## Post 3 — WhatsApp group blast (direct to school coordinator)
[2–4 short lines, casual but professional, clear action]

---
## Hashtag Suggestions
[5–8 relevant hashtags in Hebrew and/or English]

## Image Direction per Post
[Short brief for what visual to use with each post]
```

---

## Output Quality Standard
- Marketing OnePager answers: what, for whom, why different, what they get
- Video Script reads naturally in 60–90 seconds when spoken aloud
- Social posts are NOT the same message reformatted — each serves a different audience and platform
- Language matches the audience of each asset (B2B vs parent vs peer)
